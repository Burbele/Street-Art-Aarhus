Eske Touborg

Aquatic Scene

2018

  • Vestergade 62, 8000 Aarhus

Eske's impressive hyper realist mural offers an enhanced view of an aquatic scene. Thus piece's extent of realism achieves to blend into the whole view so well you can almost feel a if you're walking into a surrealist painting by just looking at it.
