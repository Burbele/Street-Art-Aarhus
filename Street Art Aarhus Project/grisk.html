Peter Birk

Nature As Alive

2015

  • Anholtsgade 5, 8000 Aarhus

Peter Birk's artistic career traces its roots all the way back to his teenage years, when he often painted graffiti in the vibrant streetscapes. During these years, art began to fill more and more space, and the desire to make a living from creative expression grew. The passion for art has meant that he is today one of Denmark's leading street art artists.
